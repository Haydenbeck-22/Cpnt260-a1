The Who

Jane is an inspired artist that expresses herself through many mediums. She loves everything from photography to music and everything in-between. On weekends you'll usually find her hiking the mountain trails for inspiration and perhaps taking some photos along the way. During the week however you'll find her dedicated to finding new ways to express herslef through her art, one day she hopes that she can share this journey with the world.

The What

Jane does commissioned photoshoots, paintings and drawings. You can contact her to find more details on booking and prices. Jane also hosts a weekly painting night at a community center, drop in's are free and supplies are provided. Come by for a night full of wine and fun! (maybe even some painting)

The Where

For photos, the commission price will be adjusted with the location. For paint night, it is located at the community center on fiction street and 7th.
